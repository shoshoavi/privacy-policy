1. Who We Are

Launch Ready ("we", "us", or "our") is a web development and digital product studio. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information submitted through our website and WhatsApp chatbot.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Full name

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

4. WhatsApp Communication

By submitting your contact details on our website, you consent to being contacted via WhatsApp by our team. Our WhatsApp chatbot is powered by AI and may process your messages to provide relevant responses. Conversation history is stored securely and accessible only to our team.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in a secure database (Supabase/PostgreSQL) with encryption at rest and in transit. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, or until you request deletion. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of WhatsApp communications at any time by replying "STOP"

8. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies for analytics and performance monitoring. No personally identifiable information is stored in cookies.
